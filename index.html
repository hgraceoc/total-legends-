ACCESS

ACCESS IS IMPORTANT TO US. NOT JUST ACCESS TO THE BEST COFFEES AND SERVICE, BUT ACCESS IN THE TRUEST SENSE OF THE WORD, BEING OPEN AND AVAILABLE TO AS MANY MEMBERS OF OUR SOCIETY AS IS POSSIBLE. SPECIALITY COFFEE SHOULDN'T BE FOR THE FEW, THOSE WHO ARE IN THE KNOW OR COFFEE CONNOISSEURS, ITS FOR EVERYONE REGARDLESS OF RACE, CREED, GENDER IDENTITY, DISABILITY OR HOWEVER YOU DEFINE YOURSELF.

WE REALISE THAT NOTHING IS PERFECT, OUR SPACE IS SMALL & ODDLY SHAPED AND A LISTED BUILDING, BUT WE ARE CONSTANTLY LOOKING AT WAYS TO WORK AROUND THIS TO REMOVE BARRIERS TO ACCESS & ARE OPEN TO FEEDBACK. WITH THIS IN MIND, THE HOTEL NEXT DOOR & THE COUNCIL OFFICE OPPOSITE ARE A HAPPY FOR OUR CUSTOMERS TO USE BOTH THEIR TOILETS & CHANGING FACILITIES.
